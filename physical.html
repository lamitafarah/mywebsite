Arduino Project
Heartbeat of Belonging
The installation consists of letters from the alphabet that are linked to a sensor capable of detecting the user's heart rate. As the user's heart rate increases, the installation responds by illuminating, visually mirroring the fluctuating rhythm of the heartbeat.
The rhythm of the heartbeat is used as a metaphor for the rhythm of life itself. In a similar way, the letters of the alphabet can be seen as the heartbeat of a country because it is the foundation of its language and communication, which are the lifeblood of a nation's culture, identity, and history.
Just as the heartbeat is crucial for sustaining the physical body, the alphabet is crucial for sustaining language and communication, an essential aspect of the vitality and spirit of a culture.
By intertwining the metaphorical rhythm of the heartbeat with the foundational elements of language and culture, this installation offers a unique and engaging experience that encourages users to reflect on their cultural roots and the importance of language in shaping their sense of identity and belonging.
