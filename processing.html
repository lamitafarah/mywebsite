Processing Projects
Learning to code in Processing has been an enjoyable experience for me. Working with Processing provides a creative outlet, allowing me to showcase my imagination through coding. I find generative art particularly fascinating, as it allows me to explore the intersection of programming and artistic expression.
A Visual Symphony of Grids
In this artwork, a foundational grid made up of squares serves as the essential building block. Upon each code execution, this base grid undergoes replication, yielding a visually mesmerizing repetition effect.Additional layers, distinguished by varying colors and orientations, are introduced. These supplementary layers are strategically placed to evoke a dynamic sense of motion and seamless continuity, creating a visually engaging and harmonious aesthetic experience.

Harmonic Exploration
Through the fusion of tangible audio visualization, dynamic movement, and user interaction, my work aims to evoke curiosity, exploration, and personal connection, inviting viewers to engage with music in a captivating way. By manipulating the keys and moving the mouse,viewers are invited to explore different shapes and combinations, allowing them to personalize their visual encounters and discover aesthetic preferences that resonate on a deeper level.

Dynamic Simulation
This digital artwork employs vector mechanics to replicate the realistic behavior of falling leaves and floating bees. This interactive scene comes to life as leaves descend and bees navigate, responding to the influence of a simulated wind activated by pressing the mouse key. Explore the precision of vector-driven dynamics, where you take control of the wind, shaping the movement of leaves and the strategic avoidance patterns of bees.
